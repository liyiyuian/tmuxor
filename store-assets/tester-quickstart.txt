TMUXor — see and continue your tmux + Claude Code sessions from your Even G2 glasses.

Running a fleet of Claude Code sessions on your computer? TMUXor puts them on your glasses so your work stays trackable, hands-free — see which session needs you, read its real prompts and replies, and reply by voice, over your own private Tailscale network. Nothing is sent to the app developer.

• Tagged fleet — each tmux window is a project tag, so you tell sessions apart at a glance and jump to the one that needs you.
• Continue your work — read the actual conversation, reopened right where you left off.
• Reply by voice — tap, speak, review the transcription, send. No OpenAI key? Type on your phone.
• Start sessions — pick a tag, name a folder, and a Claude session opens there.

Setup (one time): full guide at github.com/liyiyuian/tmuxor — run install.sh on your computer, then paste the printed config code into the app. Needs tmux + Claude Code + Tailscale.
